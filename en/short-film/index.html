I. IDEA

The group's short film was inspired by the members' love for horror films in general and the "sorcerer" genre in particular. This was also "fertile ground" with great potential, allowing the group to freely brainstorm and create content. The group made a bold creative choice by selecting the theme of sorcery and the mystical world of "Huyền Môn Đạo Thuật" as the main subject. The sorcerer film genre has always been rich with mysteries and historical lore to explore. The spiritual world has long been intertwined with the beliefs of Asian people — in ancient times, when strange phenomena could not be explained by science, people turned to the gods, building an invisible shield within themselves to cope with those mysterious occurrences.
